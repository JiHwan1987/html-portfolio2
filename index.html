My favorite board game!

My favorite board game is Go. Played on a 19x19 grid, the game unfolds as a battle between black and white stones. Within this battlefield, players must anticipate their opponent's strategies and psychology while thinking many moves ahead to secure victory. Go is an immensely complex game, with countless possibilities and patterns, making it nearly impossible to master every move. In many ways, I find the journey of a programmer to be strikingly similar to that of a Go player—both require foresight, adaptability, and a deep understanding of intricate systems.
